Cheesy Corndog Casserole

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 35 mins

Servings: 8

Description

This cheesy corndog casserole has the comforting flavors of a corndog with far less effort. Hot dogs, cheese, and corn bread mix combine in an easy casserole.

Ingredient

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 3/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 8 hot dogs, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons mustard
  • 2 teaspoons hot sauce
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages corn bread mix, such as Jiffy®
  • 8 ounces sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a 3-quart baking dish.
  2. Step 2: Heat butter in a skillet over medium-high heat until melted. Add celery and onions and cook until softened, about 4 minutes. Transfer to a mixing bowl.
  3. Step 3: Add hot dog pieces to the same skillet and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes. Transfer to mixing bowl with veggies.
  4. Step 4: Add in milk, eggs, mustard, hot sauce, cornbread mix, and 1 1/2 cups Cheddar cheese; stir until combined (it’s ok if some lumps remain). Pour into the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle any remaining cheese on top.
  5. Step 5: Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and set, 25 to 30 minutes. A toothpick inserted near the center should come out fairly clean. Let stand 5 to 10 minutes before serving.
